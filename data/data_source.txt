Dataset: E-Commerce Retail Operations — Brazil (2016–2018)
Source: Academic business case module — MSc AI & ML (Scaler/Woolf University)
Volume: 100,000+ orders across 8 tables

Tables:
- customers.csv
- orders.csv
- order_items.csv
- payments.csv
- reviews.csv
- products.csv
- sellers.csv
- geolocation.csv

Note: Raw data files are not included in this repository.
Dataset was provided as part of an academic programme and is not publicly redistributable.
